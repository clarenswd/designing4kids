Mission: Raise awareness in children P -6.

Vission: Drop the numbers of people affected by skin cancer by 2025.


Sunny Koala foundation is here to provide ways for you to keep protected in the sun while still having fun in the sea, in the park or at school!


The organisation was created in 2015 with the purpose to raise skin cancer awareness in primary school students - P - 6.


We have multiple locations where you can contact us across the QUT campus at Gardens Point. Please come and visit us, bring your parents, play games, and have fun on sunny days.
